        Backup & Restore

        Your data lives only in this browser's local storage — it isn't synced anywhere. Download a backup occasionally, especially before switching browsers, clearing site data, or moving to a new device.

        Install on Your Phone

        This app can be installed like a native app, with no app store required.

        iPhone (Safari): tap the Share icon, then "Add to Home Screen".
        Android (Chrome): tap the ⋮ menu, then "Add to Home screen" / "Install app".
